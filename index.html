The Cats of Ulthar

The sense of foreboding in the world can be used to summon entities through the veil between worlds and dimensions. These creatures generate Fear, Uncertainty and Doubt (FUD) which can be used to increase the amount of foreboding in the world.

Unit Cost Description
Tiny Horror 10 Foreboding Mischievous imps, varying in size, color and grotesquery. Generates 1 FUD per second.
Yigling 20 Foreboding Serpent children of the elder god Yig. Generates 10 FUD per second. 5% chance to generate chaos stone.
